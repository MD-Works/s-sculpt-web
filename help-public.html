Navigation bar

The bar at the top of every page lets customers jump to any section. On a phone it collapses into a ☰ menu button — they tap it to see the links.

LinkWhere it takes them
TreatmentsThe treatment cards — each shows name, price, duration and description
SpecialsThe scrolling promotions banner (you manage these in the admin)
LoyaltyThe loyalty points checker — they enter their phone number to see their balance
Find usMap, address, phone number and WhatsApp button
WhatsApp usOpens a WhatsApp chat with the salon directly
Book nowScrolls to the booking form

Hero section

The big opening banner — the first thing every visitor sees. Shows the salon name, tagline, your Google rating and two buttons: Book now and WhatsApp us.

About the Google ratingThe 4.9 ⭐ rating shown here comes from your real Google Business listing, so it's a trustworthy figure for customers.

Treatments

Shows all your active treatments as cards, split into two tabs: Sculpt (body treatments) and Face (facial treatments). Each card shows:

  • A brochure image — if you've uploaded one in the admin
  • Treatment name and price
  • Duration and session count
  • Short description
  • A Book this button that opens the booking form with that treatment pre-selected
💡 Viewing the brochure imageCustomers can click or tap the treatment image to open it full-screen for a closer look. They tap anywhere outside the image or press × to close it.
Keeping treatments currentAdd, edit or hide treatments at any time in the admin console → Treatments. Changes show on the site immediately.

Specials banner

A scrolling ticker that runs between the treatments and booking sections. It shows whatever promotional messages you've added in the admin under Specials.

It scrolls automatically, loops continuously, and pauses when a customer hovers over it on desktop. You can add as many messages as you like.

Booking form

Customers work through four steps to make a booking:

  1. Choose a treatment and date They pick a treatment from the dropdown, then a date from the calendar. Only open days are selectable — closed days and blocked dates are greyed out.
  2. Choose a time slot Available slots appear automatically based on your opening hours and existing bookings. Fully booked slots don't appear. If a treatment has multiple machines (e.g. 3 RF machines), up to 3 customers can book the same slot.
  3. Enter their details Name and phone are required. Email is optional. They can also enter a gift voucher code here — the discount applies automatically. If the voucher has more value than the treatment price, the leftover balance stays on the code for next time.
  4. Confirm They review the summary and click Confirm booking. The booking is saved instantly and appears in your admin console under Bookings. Loyalty points are added automatically.
⚠️ After a customer booksThe booking appears in your admin immediately. Loyalty points are awarded automatically (1 point per R10 of the treatment price). Voucher balances update automatically if a code was used.

Loyalty points

Customers check their balance by entering the phone number they used when booking. The page shows their current points, tier and how far they are from the next tier.

TierPoints neededBenefit
🥉 Bronze0 – 199 ptsEntry level — 1 pt per R10 spent
🥈 Silver200 – 499 pts10% off add-ons
🥇 Gold500+ ptsPriority booking + exclusive specials
💡 How points are earnedPoints are based on the full treatment price — even if a voucher was used to pay. Walk-in / cash payments: you add points manually in the admin under Customers → Add points tab.
