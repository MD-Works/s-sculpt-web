Logging in & out

Go to s-sculpt-web.pages.dev/admin.html and enter your email address and password, then click Sign in. The console opens to the Dashboard.

Forgot your password? Contact your developer to reset it.

Logging out: Click Log out at the bottom of the left sidebar. Always log out when you're finished, especially on a shared device.

⚠️ Keep your login safeOnly you should know your password. If you think someone else has access, contact your developer immediately to reset your credentials.

Dashboard Overview

The first screen after logging in. Shows three summary numbers at a glance — total bookings, active treatments, and active specials. No editing happens here, it's purely a quick overview.

Bookings Managing appointments

All bookings appear here — from the website and from the cash register. Most recent bookings are at the top.

Reading a booking card

What you seeWhat it means
Treatment nameWhat was booked
ConfirmedActive booking — client is expected
CancelledBooking was cancelled
🎁 Voucher usedA gift voucher was applied — shows how much was discounted
Date · time · priceWhen the appointment is and what was charged
Customer name · phoneWho booked
Cancel buttonCancels the booking — this cannot be undone

Cancelled bookings

Cancelled bookings are hidden at the bottom behind a toggle — "▶ X cancelled bookings — click to show". They're still there for your records, just kept out of the way.

💡 Walk-in paymentsBookings from the website appear automatically. For walk-in or cash payments, use the Customers panel → select the customer → Process payment. This records the visit and awards loyalty points in one step.
⚠️ Cancellation is permanentOnce you click Cancel on a booking it cannot be undone. Make sure before you click.

Customers Client profiles & cash register

Your full client list — everyone who has booked or been added manually. Search by name or phone number using the box at the top.

Adding a new walk-in client

  1. Click "+ Add new client"Button is at the top right of the customer list.
  2. Enter their detailsName and phone are required. Email is optional.
  3. Click Add clientThey appear in the list immediately, ready to use.

Viewing a customer profile

Click anywhere on a customer card to open their profile drawer. The stats bar at the top shows their total bookings, total spend and current points balance. Three tabs:

TabWhat you see
BookingsFull booking history — treatment, date, amount, voucher use
LoyaltyEvery points transaction — earned, adjusted, deducted — with date and reason
Add pointsManual points form — for walk-in Yoco payments, referrals, promotions or corrections

Processing a walk-in payment (cash register)

  1. Find the customerSearch by name or phone. If they're new, add them first.
  2. Open their profileClick their card.
  3. Click "Process payment"Top right of the profile drawer.
  4. Select the treatmentPrice fills in automatically from the treatments list.
  5. Apply a voucher (optional)Enter the code and click Apply. Discount and remaining balance show immediately.
  6. Calculate changeEnter the cash received. The change to give the customer appears automatically.
  7. Click Confirm paymentThe visit is recorded, voucher balance updated if used, and loyalty points awarded automatically (1 pt per R10 of treatment price).

Adding loyalty points manually

Open the customer's profile → Add points tab. Enter the number of points (positive to add, negative to deduct), choose a reason from the dropdown, add a note if helpful, then click Add points.

ReasonWhen to use it
Walk-in / Yoco paymentCustomer paid cash or Yoco card machine — use the cash register instead when possible
ReferralCustomer referred a friend who came in
PromotionBonus points as part of a special offer
CorrectionFixing a mistake in the points balance
OtherAnything else — add a note to explain

Vouchers Gift vouchers

Vouchers are generated by you after receiving payment. Customers cannot generate their own. Each voucher has a unique code the recipient uses at checkout on the website.

Generating a new voucher

  1. Fill in the detailsAmount (required), recipient name (required), from (optional), message (optional).
  2. Click "Generate voucher code"A unique code is created (e.g. SCULPT-GLOW-ROSE-247) and a branded image pops up showing the design.
  3. Share the imageOn your phone: tap Share to send directly via WhatsApp. On a computer: click Download image and attach it manually.

Voucher statuses

StatusMeaning
ActiveFull balance available — hasn't been used yet
PartialBeen used but has a remaining balance (e.g. R150 remaining of R500)
RedeemedFully used — collapsed at the bottom of the list
💡 Partial vouchersIf a R600 voucher is used on a R450 treatment, R150 stays on the same code. The customer uses the same code again for their next visit. You'll see "R150 remaining of R600" on the voucher card.
⚠️ Only generate after receiving paymentGenerating a voucher creates a real, usable code immediately. Only do this once you've received cash, EFT or card payment from the person buying the voucher.

Treatments Managing your services

Controls what appears on the public site's treatment cards. Changes take effect immediately.

Adding a new treatment

  1. Click "+ Add treatment"
  2. Fill in the details Name — what it's called on the site.
    Price — in rand (e.g. 450).
    Duration — how long one session takes in minutes.
    Sessions — number of sessions in the package (usually 1).
    Stations — how many machines you have for this treatment. Set to 1 unless you have multiple (e.g. 3 RF machines = enter 3). This allows multiple clients to book the same time slot.
    Description — shown on the treatment card. Keep it clear and informative.
    Category — Sculpt (body) or Face.
  3. Upload a brochure image (optional)Click the file picker and choose a photo. It uploads automatically and appears on the treatment card on the public site. Customers can click the image to view it full-screen.
  4. Click SaveThe treatment appears on the public site immediately.

Editing an existing treatment

Click Edit on any treatment card. Change whatever you need and click Save. To upload a new brochure image, just choose a new file — it replaces the old one.

Hiding vs deleting

ActionWhat it doesWhen to use it
HideRemoves from public site but keeps in your recordsTemporarily unavailable treatment
DeletePermanently removes the treatmentOnly if you'll never offer it again
⚠️ Deleting is permanentPast bookings for the treatment stay in the system, but the treatment itself is gone forever. Use Hide instead if you're unsure.

Specials Promotions ticker

Controls the scrolling banner that runs across the public site. Add as many messages as you like — they all scroll one after another.

  • Click Add special → type your message → Save
  • Toggle the switch on/off to show or hide a message without deleting it
  • Delete a message permanently with the bin icon
Ideas for specials"20% off all RF sessions in July"  ·  "Refer a friend and earn 100 bonus points"  ·  "Book 5 sessions, get 1 free"  ·  "New: Face Rejuvenation packages available — ask us"

Hours Opening hours & blocked dates

Controls which dates and time slots are available to book on the public site.

Setting opening hours

  • Toggle each day open or closed
  • Set opening and closing times for open days
  • Changes take effect immediately — no customer can book outside these hours

Blocking specific dates

Use this to close the salon on specific days — public holidays, training, holidays, etc.

  1. Enter the dateUse the date picker in the blocked dates section.
  2. Click AddThe date is blocked — customers will see it greyed out on the calendar.
  3. To unblockClick the × next to the date in the blocked dates list.

Database Technical information

This panel is for your developer. It shows the Supabase project ID, security policy details and database structure. You don't need to use this panel for day-to-day management — leave it alone.
